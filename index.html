About Me

I am a Syrian immigrant. I left Syria in 2012 looking for a safer place to call home. I traveled around the Middle East, Europe, and Asia before settling in the United States.
My curiosity and passion for learning led me to pursue a career in Data Science, particularly projects related to machine learning and neural networks. When not coding, I enjoy the outdoors by running, hiking, and camping. I am eager to expand my knowledge in deep learning and computer vision, as well as work on projects with results relevant in society.
